Pushlane – Support

Contact support, report a bug, or ask a question about the Pushlane mobile application.

Last updated: 17 December 2025

If you need help with Pushlane, you can contact us anytime using the email below. To help us respond faster, please include your device model, OS version, and Pushlane app version.

Support email: vaycepro@gmail.com
Tip: In your email subject, you can write something like Bug, Subscription, or Feature request.

1. Quick help

1.1 Subscriptions

  • “Offer unavailable. Try again later.”: please check your internet connection, close and reopen the app, then try again.
  • Restore purchases: open the Premium screen in the app and tap Restore purchases (if available).

1.2 Widgets

  • Widget not updating: reboot your device, remove the widget, add it again, then open the app once.

1.3 Notifications

  • Notifications not firing: check your device settings → Notifications → Pushlane is allowed.

2. What to include in your message

To help us troubleshoot quickly, please include:

  • Your device (e.g., iPhone / iPad model)
  • Your OS version (e.g., iOS 26.1)
  • Your Pushlane app version (e.g., 1.0 build 10)
  • What you were doing when the issue happened
  • Any error message you saw
  • Screenshots or a short video (optional but helpful)
