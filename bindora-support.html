1. Quick help

Scanning documents

Make sure your camera permission is enabled. Place the document on a flat surface, use good lighting, and avoid strong shadows for cleaner scans.

OCR and search

If text recognition does not work as expected, try scanning again with a sharper image. OCR quality depends on lighting, focus, and document readability.

Reminders

If reminders do not appear, check that notifications are allowed for Bindora in your device settings.

Document organization

Use folders, tags, and clear document names to keep bills, warranties, receipts, contracts, and important papers easy to find.

2. Common troubleshooting steps

2.1 Camera permission

  • Open your device settings.
  • Find Bindora.
  • Make sure camera access is enabled.

2.2 Notifications

  • Open your device notification settings.
  • Make sure notifications are allowed for Bindora.
  • If reminders still do not appear, restart your device and reopen the app.

2.3 OCR accuracy

  • Use a clear, well-lit document.
  • Avoid blurry photos, folded paper, reflections, and strong shadows.
  • For small text, try scanning closer while keeping the document fully visible.

2.4 Missing or hard-to-find documents

  • Use the search bar with a word, date, amount, issuer, or document name.
  • Check folders and tags if the document was moved after scanning.
  • If the document was recently added, reopen the app and try searching again.

2.5 Purchases or subscriptions

  • Make sure you are connected to the internet.
  • Close and reopen Bindora, then try again.
  • If available, use the Restore purchases option inside the app.

4. Privacy and data

Bindora is designed to help you keep important documents organized and easy to find. We take privacy seriously and aim to keep the experience simple, transparent, and secure.

Do not send sensitive documents by email unless support specifically asks for a sample and you are comfortable sharing it. If needed, hide or blur personal details before sending screenshots.
